About Me

I'm Diego Crisafulli, a double major in Computational Arts and Computer Science at Concordia University. My journey into technology started with 3D modeling and quickly evolved into exploring immersive simulations, AI-driven solutions, and VR/AR experiences.

I'm passionate about bridging the gap between artistic creativity and engineering to develop sustainable solutions that have a meaningful impact on our world. Whether it's leveraging machine learning for advanced analytics, or creating interactive VR worlds for education and neuroscience, I constantly seek to push boundaries and learn new techniques.

My Vision

My long-term goal is to pioneer clean, immersive technologies that both entertain and drive positive environmental change. From generative AI to real-time simulations, I'm always exploring how technology can inspire and improve lives.
