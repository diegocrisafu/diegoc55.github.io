About Me

Hello! I’m Diego Crisafulli, a Computational Arts and Computer Science specialist dedicated to building **interactive, AI-powered experiences**.

My work spans **machine learning**, **3D modeling**, and **immersive simulations**—leveraging technologies like **VR/AR**, **Generative AI**, and **physics-based engines**. Through my projects, I aim to **push the boundaries** of how we experience digital worlds and contribute meaningful solutions to **green initiatives**.

When I’m not coding or modeling 3D environments, you’ll find me **researching** the latest innovations in sustainability tech, **painting**, or **exploring** new ways to integrate art and technology for a positive environmental impact.
